About Wild Rescues

Wild Rescues is a nonprofit organization dedicated to rescuing injured, orphaned, and displaced wildlife. Our team of volunteers works hard to rehabilitate animals and safely release them back into their natural habitat.

We believe every wild animal deserves a second chance. Through rescue, rehabilitation, and education, we help protect wildlife and raise awareness about the importance of conservation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue and care for wildlife in need, provide medical treatment and rehabilitation, and release healthy animals back into the wild. We also educate the public about wildlife protection and environmental responsibility.

Volunteer Opportunities

Wild Rescues relies on volunteers to help care for animals, maintain habitats, and assist with community outreach. If you are passionate about wildlife, consider joining our volunteer team.
